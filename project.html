Pet Care Central

Pet Care Central is a DBMS designed to help pet shop owners manage data efficiently.

Focused on admin functionalities, it stores information about animals, birds, products, and customer contact details, eliminating the need for manual record-keeping.

The system streamlines operations by allowing easy data entry, inventory tracking, and sales management, all in one place.

This project significantly impacts pet shops by automating tasks, reducing errors, and improving data accuracy.

It helps shop owners make informed decisions, improve customer service, and focus on growing their business with greater efficiency.

Sky Cast - Weather Forecasting Application

Sky Cast: is a dynamic project designed to provide real-time weather updates and forecasts.

It retrieves weather data from reliable APIs, offering users accurate information on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and future weather conditions for any location.

The user-friendly interface ensures easy navigation, making weather updates accessible at a glance.

This project impacts users by helping them plan their day based on current and future weather conditions.
